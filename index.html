👥 Who plays together? ▼
  • There are 7 mixed teams.
  • Each team has 1 student from PGHS (Pottsgrove High School, Pennsylvania) and 1 to 3 students from AEG (Albert-Einstein-Gymnasium, Sankt Augustin).
  • The PGHS student is your American exchange partner — treat them as your guide to American culture during the bonus tasks!
  • All team members explore the school together the whole time. Nobody splits up.
✨ Choosing your team name ▼
Your team name must be made of exactly one English word + one German word, combined into one creative name.
💡 Examples: "wunderbar together" · "Abenteuer friends" · "spitze crew" · "Freunde forever" · "schön vibes"
You will enter your team name in the next screen. Both words should feel connected — be creative!
🕐 Time & scoring ▼
  • You have 30 minutes from the moment your team starts.
  • Each solved task earns +10 points. Bonus tasks earn +15 points.
  • The team with the most points wins. If teams tie on points, the one who finished faster wins.
  • You can tackle tasks in any order — no fixed route!
  • When your 30 minutes are up the timer stops automatically and your score is saved.
📱 Using your phone ▼
  • Tasks marked 📱 Phone OK — you are allowed and encouraged to search the web.
  • Tasks marked 📸 Photo Required — you must take a real photo with your phone. Tap the photo button in the app to confirm you took it.
  • Tasks with no phone badge — no Googling! Find the answer by exploring the school.
The teacher will check photo tasks at the end by reviewing your camera roll.
🏠🌳 Indoor vs. Outdoor tasks ▼
  • 🏠 Indoor tasks are solved inside the school building.
  • 🌳 Outdoor tasks require you to go outside onto the school grounds or front entrance area.
  • Stay within the school premises at all times.
⭐ Bonus tasks ▼
There are 2 bonus tasks worth 15 points each. They are harder and more creative. One involves your PGHS partner sharing something about American culture, the other requires finding a surprising fact about Pottstown. These are optional but highly recommended for winning!
🌐 About GAPP ▼
This exchange is proudly sponsored by the German American Partnership Program (GAPP), which has connected German and American students since 1983. GAPP supports thousands of students every year in experiencing each other's cultures firsthand. You are part of that tradition today! 🎉
